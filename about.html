Our Story

About Us – Bintumani Young Stars Football Club
Founded: 2017
Founder: Sadiku Sallieu Fofanah (Coach Kanayo)
Sole Sponsor: Mantenkelly Fofanah & Marie Our Story The current name was given by our club manager and sponsor, Mantenkelly Fofanah, with full support from his wife, Marie. This dedicated couple has been the backbone of our club, providing sponsorship and unwavering support from day one.
Our Mission
At Bintumani Young Stars, our mission is to:
Scout and develop young football talent
Create opportunities on national and international stages
Uplift young players from street life through sport
Build discipline, confidence, and career pathways for youth,
We proudly serve as a home for many rising stars, building a future where talent is discovered, nurtured, and celebrated.
